Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How old do I need to be to rent a car?

A: You must be at least 21 years old to rent a car with us.

Q: What documents do I need to provide for renting a car?

A: You will need to provide a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name, and a form of identification (e.g., passport or ID card).

Q: Can I add an additional driver to my rental?

A: Yes, you can add an additional driver for an extra fee. The additional driver must meet our age and license requirements.

Q: Do you offer insurance coverage for the rental cars?

A: Yes, we offer various insurance coverage options for our rental cars. You can choose the coverage that best suits your needs.

Q: What happens if I return the car late?

A: If you return the car after the agreed-upon return time, you may be subject to additional charges. It's important to notify us in advance if you anticipate any delays.

Q: Can I cancel my reservation?

A: Yes, you can cancel your reservation. However, please note that cancellation fees may apply depending on the timing of the cancellation. Refer to our cancellation policy for more details.
